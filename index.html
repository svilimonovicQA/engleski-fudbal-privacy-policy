Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-21

This Privacy Policy explains how Engleski Fudbal ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Engleski Fudbal mobile application.

1. Who We Are

App owner and legal entity: Engleski Fudbal
Contact email: engleski.fudbal@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information (Firebase Authentication): Email address, authentication identifiers, and account status (for example, whether your email is verified).
  • Profile Information (Firebase Firestore): Display name, username, soccer username, selected favorite club, profile photo URL (if you upload one), user ID, and account creation metadata.
  • User Content/Data You Provide: Profile photo uploads (stored in Firebase Storage).
  • App Activity and Analytics (Firebase Analytics): Events such as screen views, login/sign-up method, content interactions, search terms, and quiz-related actions (e.g., quiz start, answers, score, completion timing).
  • Technical/Device Data: App/runtime and device-level analytics data made available through Firebase Analytics in supported native environments.
  • Content Requests: The app fetches public football/news content from the Engleski Fudbal WordPress API.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts and authentication sessions.
  • To provide core features (profile, quiz participation and results, leaderboard-related experiences, content browsing).
  • To personalize app experience (for example, favorite club features).
  • To maintain service quality, monitor usage, and improve app functionality and performance.
  • To support account security flows, including email verification and password reset.

4. Legal Basis and Consent

We process data as needed to provide app functionality you request (for example, account login and profile services), for our legitimate interests in improving and securing the app, and based on your actions/consent where required by applicable law.
